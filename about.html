About Us

We are the world's largest university

Eduford University was established in Colombo, Sri Lanka in 1990 as the first private higher education institute awarding reputed British degrees in the field of Information Communication Technology (ICT) and professional business. Eduford University has reshaped and revolutionize the higher education sector of Sri Lanka by empowering graduates to become innovators, entrepreneurs and business leaders. Over the course of three decades, Eduford University has established itself as a pioneer, providing British higher education. Currently, Eduford University has produced more than 3000 graduates who are spread across the globe, playing pivotal roles in well reputed companies.
